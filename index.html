SmartList Documentations

SmartList is a mailing list management (MLM; listserv) software, which is built on top of Procmail, and is maintained by the Procmail developers. This site hosts the SmartList FAQ along with several important documentations, include:

  1. The SmartList Manual by the original SmartList author, Stephen R. van den Berg.
  2. The SmartList FAQ (with 73 Frequently Asked Questions; last updated on May 26, 2021.)

The FAQ was originally written by Jason Kohles (jason@mindwell.com), modified/ enhanced by Werner Reisberger (werner@pure.ch), updated and previously maintained by Peter Hartzler (pete@hartzler.net), and Zhiliang Hu (2005-2022).

Many items in this FAQ are contributed by users like you. If you have items to contribute, suggestions or questions for attention, we encourage you to post them on the Smartlist User's mailing list (Alt site) this way your item gets a "free review" by many sharp eyes/minds. The discussion archive on the list since the year 2000 can be found here.
